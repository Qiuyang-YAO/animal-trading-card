Snow Leopard

Snow leopard attacks on humans are rare. They prefer to eat plants rather than herders' livestocks when they have enough to eat.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera uncia
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Population: Less than 10,000
  • Habitat: Alpine

The snow leopard, also known as the ounce, is a large cat native to the mountain ranges of Central and South Asia. It is listed as Vulnerable on the IUCN Red List of Threatened Species because the global population is estimated to number less than 10,000 mature individuals and decline about 10% in the next 23 years. It is threatened by poaching and habitat destruction following infrastructural developments. (from wikipedia)
